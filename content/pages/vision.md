# Your Brain Deserves Better

Doomscrolling is shrinking your focus. The constant barrage of short-form videos, endless feeds, and algorithmic notifications are hijacking your brain's reward system. You might find yourself losing track of thoughts mid-sentence or struggling to concentrate on a single task for more than a few minutes. This is the reality of the AI-era memory loss.

## The Science

When you doomscroll, your brain receives artificial dopamine hits. These quick, unearned rewards train your brain to seek constant stimulation, weakening your prefrontal cortex—the area responsible for sustained attention and impulse control. Your working memory suffers, and your cognitive flexibility declines.

## Our Solution

DopaMind is your daily brain gym. We've designed 45-second micro-workouts that stimulate positive focus feedback loops. Instead of mindless scrolling, you engage in intentional cognitive exercises that train:

- **Quick Reflexes**: Processing speed and reaction time.
- **Remember & Recall**: Spatial and working memory.
- **Think & Solve**: Mental agility and math skills.
- **Word Power**: Cognitive flexibility.

By committing to a daily streak, you build a habit of positive focus, gradually restoring your attention span.
