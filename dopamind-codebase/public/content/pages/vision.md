# Vision: Gamifying Brain Growth

We believe that **games are the ultimate weapon against doomscrolling**.

From playing digital video games to cricket, the human brain is naturally attracted to play, challenge, and reward. 

Instead of fighting your brain's natural wiring, we harness it. **DopaMind is a research-oriented, data-driven platform** that builds specialized cognitive games designed to stimulate brain growth and beat algorithmic scrolling addiction.

We don't build addictive algorithms. We build cognitive resistance to them.
