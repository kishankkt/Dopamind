# v0.1.0 — Initial Release

We are thrilled to launch the very first version of DopaMind! This release establishes the core platform and introduces the initial set of brain training micro-workouts.

## What's New

- **Core Game Engine**: A performant, zero-dependency game loop rendering at 60fps.
- **15 Mini-Games**: The initial library of 45-second cognitive workouts:
  - *SpeedMatch*, *ReactionTap*, *DirectionDash*, *ChromaShift* (Quick Reflexes)
  - *PatternPulse*, *SymbolMatch*, *EchoMap* (Remember & Recall)
  - *FocusGrid*, *TimeEstimator*, *PhaseLock* (Stay Sharp)
  - *CountFlow*, *NumberCascade*, *WeightGuess* (Think & Solve)
  - *WordWarp* (Word Power)
  - *GravitySort* (Sort & Prioritize)
- **Daily Streak System**: Plant and grow your digital pixel plant by completing daily workouts.
- **Performance Dashboard**: Track your reaction times, accuracy, and overall score across sessions.
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Available instantly on the web, with desktop binaries for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
- **Privacy-First Authentication**: Secure, email-based and Google OAuth login via Supabase, with zero third-party tracking.
