# Welcome to DopaMind

Today, we're excited to introduce DopaMind, your daily brain gym. We built DopaMind because we realized our own attention spans were shrinking. We found ourselves reaching for our phones out of habit, doomscrolling through endless feeds, and struggling to maintain focus on deep work.

We wanted a solution that wasn't another productivity tracker or a complex clinical tool. We wanted something simple, fast, and effective.

## 45 Seconds to Better Focus

The core of DopaMind is a collection of 15 micro-workouts designed to be played in just 45 seconds. These aren't just random games; they are carefully crafted cognitive exercises targeting specific areas of brain function:

- **Quick Reflexes**: Games like *SpeedMatch* and *ReactionTap* to improve processing speed.
- **Remember & Recall**: Games like *PatternPulse* and *EchoMap* to strengthen working memory.
- **Stay Sharp**: Games like *FocusGrid* and *PhaseLock* to enhance sustained attention.

## A Privacy-First Gym

Your focus is personal, and so is your data. DopaMind is built with a zero-bloat, privacy-first philosophy. We don't use tracking pixels, we don't serve ads, and your data is securely stored.
