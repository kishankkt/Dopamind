# 🔒 DopaMind Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 3, 2026

Welcome to **DopaMind**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, handle, and store your data when you use the DopaMind desktop, mobile, or web application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

DopaMind collects data to optimize your cognitive training and process payments securely.

* **Account Credentials:** Managed entirely via **Supabase Auth**. We do not store plain-text passwords on our servers.
* **Game Performance Data:** High scores, streak counts, daily activity timestamps, and reaction speed metrics.
* **Payment Data:** Transactions are processed securely via third-party providers (**Stripe** and **Razorpay**). DopaMind does not store or see your credit card details; we only receive secure transaction tokens and subscription status webhooks.
* **Device Information:** Native OS type (Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, or iOS), app version, and screen resolution to ensure responsive graphics.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
* Customize your cognitive difficulty parameters (e.g. adaptive speed scaling).
* Maintain your daily streak metrics (Pixel Plant growth).
* Facilitate secure micro-transactions and subscription billing.
* Improve game layout, frame rate stability, and UX.

---

## 3. Data Protection & Sharing

* **No Data Sales:** We do not sell or rent user data to advertisers or third parties.
* **Service Providers:** We share limited information with Supabase (backend databases) and Stripe/Razorpay (payment processing) to fulfill basic application operations.
* **Security:** We employ encryption at rest and in transit via HTTPS for all communication between the native desktop shells and serverless endpoints.

---

## 4. Compliance (GDPR & CCPA)

* **Right of Erasure:** You can request the deletion of your account and all associated gameplay logs at any time directly through the app settings or by contacting our support team.
* **Access to Data:** You have the right to export your training history and account profile information.

For inquiries regarding data privacy, contact us at: **privacy@dopamind.app**
